Pirates!

  • Create a ship: exampleShip = new PirateShip()
  • Add a pirate: exampleShip.add(new Pirate("ExampleDude", new Mate()))
  • Make at least 3 pirates and you can vote on a Captain: exampleShip.chooseCaptain()
  • The Captain can choose a First Mate: exampleShip.crew[indexOfCaptain].role.chooseFirstMate(exampleShip)
  • The Captain can also command mates to walk the plank: exampleShip.crew[indexOfCaptain].role.walkPlankCommand(exampleShip, "exampleDude")
  • Watch out, Captain! The First Mate can attempt a mutiny! (not working yet): exampleShip.crew[indexOfFirstMate].role.attemptMutiny(exampleShip)
