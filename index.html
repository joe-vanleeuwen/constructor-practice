Pirates!

  • Create a ship: exampleShip = new PirateShip()
  • Add a pirate: exampleShip.add($.extend(new Pirate("exampleDude"), mateAttrs);
  • Make at least 3 pirates and you can vote on a Captain: exampleShip.chooseCaptain()
  • The Captain can choose a First Mate: exampleShip.captain.chooseFirstMate()
  • The Captain can also command mates to walk the plank: exampleShip.captain.walkPlankCommand("exampleDude")
  • Watch out, Captain! The First Mate can attempt a mutiny!: exampleShip.firstMate.attemptMutiny()
